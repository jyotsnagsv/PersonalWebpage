Teaching experience during graduate studies

I am very much interested in teaching and strongly believe that concepts can be made accessible with intent, receptivity to feedback, and the right use of teaching tools. This page contains a brief summary of my experience in teaching during my graduate studies at UC San Diego.

Teaching roles

  • Instructor: Preparing lessons and delivering lectures, preparing assignments and exams, planning course logistics, coordinating with teaching assistant, reviewing feedback from students
  • Teaching Assistant: Holding office hours (and sometimes discussion sections), grading assignments, reviewing exams
  • Lab Teaching Assistant Coordinator: Training teaching assistants, holding office hours, coordinating graders and overseeing grading, reviewing lab materials
